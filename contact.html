Communications-related facts about Maryland:

  • The first Post Office system in the United States was inaugurated in Baltimore in 1774.
  • The first telegraph line in the world was established in 1844 and ran between Washington, D.C. and Baltimore.
  • The Maryland Gazette is the oldest continuously published newspaper in the United States and was founded in 1727.
